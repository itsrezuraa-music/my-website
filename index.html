About
Rezura

Rezura is a solo artist creating at the intersection of Pop, R&B, and Alternative — delivering a sound that is intimate, honest, and deeply felt.

Every song is a conversation: with oneself, with memories, with everything that was never said out loud. Music made to be felt, not just heard.
